Cycle 3 contains our third project for Digital Fabrication. We did not learn a new software, but instead used a new machine. The vinyl cutter. Using Adobe Illustrator to create an image of our own design to implement on apparel or any surface as a sticker.

For my project, I created one design of a simple image with a quote. The image is a mountain range created by drawing some shapes on Adobe Illustrator and adding different colors to the different layers. The sticker on my lap top is the origional design, while the vinyl on the shirt is an alteration of the design.

I found the vinyl cutter to be harder to use than the 3-D printers. However, after being shown multiple times and using the machine myself I soon got a hang up creating my design. The sticker on my lap top came out perfect compared to the apparel vinyl.

I had some trouble using the apparel vinyl when cutting the vinyl. The ratio was not equal for the inner vinyl piece leaving only the outline of the mountains and written words. The design still came out better than I would have hoped.
