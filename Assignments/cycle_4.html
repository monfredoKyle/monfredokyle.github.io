Cycle 4 contains our fourth project for Digital Fabrication. We were required to learn a new software. TinkerCAD and Arguino. Both pieces of software were relatively easy to understand and straight forward. The coding was different than previous projects since TinkerCAD uses blocks to code in arguino and writes the arguino code for you.

The project asked for students to create a circuit first on TinkerCAD than on a bread board. The circuit had to have three individual LEDs operate on their own and contain one button. The circuit I chose to build had three LEDs operate as a group and on their own. My circuit also had four buttons and seven resistors.
